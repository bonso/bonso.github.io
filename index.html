Bonso is a legal non-for-profit based in the Louisville area that believes in creating the needed change in society. In order to generate the funds to Benefit Others Needing Support for Opportunities, Bonso sells affordable, high quality promotional products to its consumers while designating a portion of the sales revenue to the community through either a scholarship or donation. The company also provides invaluable, real-world experience to our student workers by providing the unique opportunity to operate in each division of the business structure.
